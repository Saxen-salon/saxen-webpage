# Service Pages — Content Requirements

## Services Hub Page

### Page Goal
Show the full range of capabilities at a glance. Help visitors find the specific service they need. Position the company as a full-service provider.

### Required Content Elements

| Element | Priority | Content Needed |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Capability overview | Required | Brief intro positioning the company's service breadth |
| Service grid/cards | Required | All service categories with name, image/icon, short description, link to detail |
| Cross-service value | Recommended | Why having all these capabilities under one roof matters to the customer |
| CTA | Required | "Discuss your project" or equivalent |

## Individual Service Detail Page

### Page Goal
Prove technical competence in this specific service. Convince a technical evaluator that the company can handle their project. Drive quote/inquiry conversion.

### Required Content Elements

| Element | Priority | Content Needed |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Service name + description | Required | What this service is, what problems it solves, who it's for |
| Process steps | Required | How the service works — step-by-step with enough detail to build confidence |
| Capabilities / specs | Required | Specific technical capabilities, equipment, tolerances, materials, etc. |
| Quality approach | Recommended | How quality is ensured in this specific service |
| Related services | Recommended | Cross-links to complementary services |
| Case study excerpt | Recommended | 1-2 examples of this service in action, linking to full case studies |
| FAQ | Optional | Common questions specific to this service |
| CTA | Required | Quote/consultation request with context-specific copy |

### Technical Data Presentation
When a service involves technical specifications:
- Use comparison tables where relevant
- Include units and ranges, not just single values
- Link to relevant standards or certifications
- Make data scannable — don't bury specs in prose
