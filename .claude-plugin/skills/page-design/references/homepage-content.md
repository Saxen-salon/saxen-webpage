# Homepage — Content Requirements

## Page Goal
Establish the company as the right choice. Route visitors to the right section based on their intent. Convert awareness into inquiry.

## Audiences
- **Technical evaluators** assessing capability → route to Services
- **Procurement professionals** comparing suppliers → route to Cases, Contact
- **Decision makers** assessing credibility → show Trust indicators, About

## Required Content Elements

### Primary Value Proposition
- **What:** Single clear statement of what the company does and why they're the right choice
- **Must communicate:** Core specialty + geography + key differentiator + heritage
- **CTA:** Primary (Get in Touch / Request Quote) and secondary (Explore Services)

### Trust Indicators
- **What:** Key credibility metrics displayed prominently
- **Data points:** Years in business, production capacity, team size, certifications, key capabilities
- **Purpose:** Instant credibility for first-time visitors

### Services Overview
- **What:** Summary of core service capabilities
- **Content:** Service categories with brief descriptions
- **Each service needs:** Name, 1-2 sentence description, link to detail page
- **Purpose:** Show breadth of capability; help visitors find relevant service

### Proof of Capability
- **What:** Featured case studies or client outcomes
- **Content:** 2-4 case studies showing different industries/applications
- **Each case needs:** Industry label, project title, one key metric, link to full case
- **Purpose:** Move from claims to evidence

### Final Conversion Prompt
- **What:** Clear call-to-action with promise (e.g., "Response within 24 hours")
- **CTAs:** Contact form + direct phone/email

## SEO Requirements
- Single H1 containing primary keyword
- Internal links to all major sections
- Organization + LocalBusiness schema
- Image alt text on all visual elements

## Content That Does NOT Belong on Homepage
- Detailed technical specifications (belongs on service pages)
- Full company history (belongs on about page)
- Pricing information
- Long-form educational content (belongs on dedicated pages)
